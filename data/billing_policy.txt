BILLING POLICY AND REFUND PROCEDURES
=====================================

Last Updated: January 2025

## Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit cards
- PayPal
- ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only)
- Wire transfer (Enterprise, annual billing only)

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers.

---

## Billing Cycles

- Monthly plans are billed on the same day each month (anniversary date).
- Annual plans are billed once per year at a 20% discount.
- Invoices are emailed automatically to the account's registered billing email address.
- You can view and download all past invoices in **Settings > Billing > Invoice History**.

---

## Duplicate Charges

If you notice duplicate charges on your billing statement:
1. Log into your account and go to **Settings > Billing > Transaction History**.
2. Compare the transaction dates and amounts.
3. Duplicate charges are typically caused by payment retry events or plan upgrade timing.
4. Contact support at billing@support.com with:
   - Your account email
   - The duplicate transaction IDs
   - The date(s) of the charges
5. Our billing team will investigate within 2 business days and issue a refund if confirmed.

---

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility for Refunds
- Refunds are available within 14 days of the charge date for monthly subscriptions.
- Annual subscription refunds are prorated based on unused months.
- Refunds are NOT issued for partial months, API usage overages, or add-on services already consumed.

### How to Request a Refund
1. Email billing@support.com with subject line: "Refund Request - [Your Account ID]"
2. Include the transaction ID and reason for the refund request.
3. Refund requests are reviewed within 3-5 business days.
4. Approved refunds are credited back to the original payment method within 5-10 business days.

### Automatic Refund Triggers
The following situations result in automatic refunds without requiring a request:
- Service outages exceeding 4 hours in a billing period (credit applied)
- Billing errors caused by platform system issues
- Cancellations within the 3-day trial period

---

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancel your subscription anytime from **Settings > Billing > Cancel Plan**.
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period.
- No refunds are issued for the remaining days after cancellation unless within the 14-day refund window.
- Data is retained for 30 days post-cancellation before permanent deletion.

---

## Billing Disputes

For formal billing disputes:
- All billing disputes must be submitted within 60 days of the charge date.
- Disputes submitted after 60 days may not be eligible for review.
- Enterprise customers have a dedicated billing representative for dispute resolution.
- Estimated resolution time for billing disputes: 5-10 business days.
